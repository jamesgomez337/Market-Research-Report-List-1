<p><p>The four-way valve market includes various types designed for controlling fluid flow in multiple directions. The ball valve utilizes a spherical closure element to enable or block flow, providing quick and reliable operation. The piston valve, featuring a movable piston, adjusts flow with precision, ideal for high-pressure applications. Other types include butterfly valves and gate valves, each serving specific industrial needs. Collectively, these varieties enhance system efficiency in sectors like oil and gas, water treatment, and manufacturing.</p></p>
