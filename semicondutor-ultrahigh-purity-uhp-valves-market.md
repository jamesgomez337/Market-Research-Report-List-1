<p><p>The Semiconductor Ultrahigh Purity (UHP) Valves market is categorized into two main types: Low Flow and High Flow valves. Low Flow valves are designed for applications that require precise control of gas or liquid flow in small volumes, crucial for sensitive semiconductor processes. In contrast, High Flow valves cater to applications needing larger volumes, facilitating rapid gas delivery in manufacturing environments. Both types ensure minimal contamination and maintain the integrity of ultra-pure media essential for semiconductor fabrication.</p></p>
