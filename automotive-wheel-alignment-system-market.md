<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Automotive Wheel Alignment System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Toe- In / Toe-Out Alignment System</li><li>Camber Alignment System</li><li>Caster Alignment System</li><li>Four Wheel Alignment System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive wheel alignment system market includes various types focused on enhancing vehicle handling and tire longevity. Toe-in and toe-out alignment systems adjust the angle of the wheels relative to the centerline, impacting steering response. Camber alignment systems manage the tilt of the wheels, affecting contact with the road surface. Caster alignment systems adjust the angle of the steering pivot, improving stability. Four-wheel alignment systems coordinate all four wheels for optimal performance, ensuring balanced handling and improved tire wear.</p></p>
