<p><strong>The Blind Flanges Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>FF</li><li>RF</li><li>MFM</li><li>TG</li><li>RJ</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The blind flanges market is categorized into various types based on their design and function. Flat Face (FF) flanges are used with flat surfaces, while Raised Face (RF) flanges have a small raised area for better sealing. Male-Female (MFM) flanges feature interlocking edges, enhancing alignment and sealing. Tongue and Groove (TG) flanges provide a secure, aligned fit for piping systems. Lastly, Ring Joint (RJ) flanges utilize a metal ring for high-pressure applications, ensuring leak-proof connections in critical environments.</p></p>
