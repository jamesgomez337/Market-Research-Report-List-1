<p><p>Intelligent Power Modules (IPMs) are crucial components in power electronics, integrating power devices with control circuits for efficient energy management. The IGBT-IPM market focuses on insulated-gate bipolar transistors, ideal for high-voltage applications like motor drives and industrial power systems. In contrast, the MOSFET-IPM market utilizes metal-oxide-semiconductor field-effect transistors, favored for low-voltage and faster-switching applications, such as consumer electronics and renewable energy systems. Both types enhance performance, reliability, and thermal management in various electronic applications.</p></p>
