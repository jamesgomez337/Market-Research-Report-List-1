<p><ul><li>Monopole Foundation Offshore Wind Energy Infrastructure</li><li>Floating Foundation Offshore Wind Energy Infrastructure</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Offshore wind energy infrastructure encompasses various foundation types used to support wind turbines in marine environments. Monopole foundations feature a single large column anchored to the seabed, ideal for shallow waters, providing stability and ease of installation. Floating foundations, on the other hand, are designed to support turbines in deeper waters, using buoyancy and anchoring systems to remain stable. Additional market types may include jacket foundations and gravity-based structures, catering to specific site conditions and project requirements in offshore wind development.</p></p>
