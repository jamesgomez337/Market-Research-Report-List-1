<p><p>The tomato powder market comprises various types, primarily categorized into spray-dried, freeze-dried, and other methods. Spray-dried tomato powder is produced by rapidly drying liquid tomato pulp, resulting in a lightweight product with retained flavor and nutrients, ideal for snacks and seasonings. Freeze-dried tomato powder involves freezing the pulp and then dehydrating it in a vacuum, preserving more nutrients and flavor, making it suitable for gourmet applications. Other methods may include air drying or sun drying, each offering distinct product characteristics.</p></p>
