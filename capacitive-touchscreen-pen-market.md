<p><strong>The Capacitive Touchscreen Pen Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Active Capacitive Pen</li><li>Passive Capacitive Pen</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The capacitive touchscreen pen market is primarily divided into two types: active and passive capacitive pens. Active capacitive pens utilize internal electronics, allowing for pressure sensitivity and precise input, making them ideal for tasks like drawing and note-taking. They typically require a power source and may offer added features like tilt recognition. In contrast, passive capacitive pens lack electronic components, relying on conductive materials to simulate finger input, making them simpler and more affordable but less precise than their active counterparts.</p></p>
