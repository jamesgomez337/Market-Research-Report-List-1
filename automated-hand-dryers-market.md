<p><p>The automated hand dryers market primarily consists of two types: jet air dryers and hot air dryers. Jet air dryers use high-velocity streams of air to rapidly dry hands in a few seconds, promoting hygiene with less contact. They are often energy-efficient and have a modern design. In contrast, hot air dryers emit warm air to evaporate moisture, typically taking longer to dry hands. While they are usually quieter, they may consume more energy and be less hygienic than jet air models.</p></p>
