<p><p>Amorphous silicon solar cells are categorized into two main types based on their structure: single junction and multi-junction. Single junction cells consist of a single layer of amorphous silicon, optimizing for simplicity and lower costs, though they offer lower efficiency. Multi-junction cells, on the other hand, stack multiple layers of different semiconductor materials, enhancing light absorption and overall efficiency. This allows multi-junction cells to achieve higher performance, making them suitable for specialized applications, albeit at a higher manufacturing cost.</p></p>
