<p><p>The Tuberculosis Diagnostics Market is categorized into several types: </p><p>1. **Culture-based**: This traditional method involves growing the bacteria from a patient sample in a controlled environment, allowing for the identification and drug susceptibility testing.</p><p>2. **Sputum Smear Microscopy**: A rapid, cost-effective technique that examines stained sputum samples under a microscope to detect TB bacteria.</p><p>3. **Rapid Molecular Testing**: Employs advanced technologies, such as PCR, for quick and accurate identification of TB and its resistance to drugs.</p><p>4. **Others**: This includes various emerging tests and technologies, such as serological tests and imaging techniques.</p></p>
