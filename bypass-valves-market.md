<p><p>Bypass valves are essential components in fluid control systems, designed to divert flow to maintain system pressure or protect equipment. The market includes various types: </p><p>- **Globe valves** feature a spherical body and offer precise flow regulation.</p><p>- **Piston valves** utilize a movable piston for controlled flow and pressure.</p><p>- **Gate valves** use a wedge mechanism for on/off control.</p><p>- **Diaphragm valves** use a flexible diaphragm to manage flow without leakage.</p><p>- **Slide valves** provide efficient flow control by sliding mechanisms.</p><p>- **Others** encompass specialized designs catering to niche applications.</p></p>
