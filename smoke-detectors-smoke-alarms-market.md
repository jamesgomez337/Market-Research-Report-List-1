<p><p>The smoke detectors and smoke alarms market is segmented into two primary types: ionization smoke detectors and photoelectric smoke detectors. Ionization smoke detectors are designed to quickly sense flaming fires, utilizing radioactive material to detect changes in ionization levels. Photoelectric smoke alarms, on the other hand, excel at detecting smoldering fires through light-scattering technology. Both types are essential for fire safety, serving to alert occupants to potential dangers, thus reducing the risk of fire-related incidents in residential and commercial settings.</p></p>
